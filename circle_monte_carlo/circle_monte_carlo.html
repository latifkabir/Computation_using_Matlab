CIRCLE_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Circle Circumference Integrals

CIRCLE_MONTE_CARLO is a MATLAB library which estimates the integral of F(X,Y) along the circumference of the unit circle in 2D.

Languages:

CIRCLE_MONTE_CARLO is available in a C version and a C++ version and a FORTRAN77 version and a FORTRAN90 version and a MATLAB version.

Source Code:

  • circle01_length.m, returns the length of the circumference of the unit circle in 2D.
  • circle01_monomial_integral.m, integrates a monomial on the circumference of the unit circle in 2D.
  • circle01_sample.m, samples points from the circumference of the unit circle in 2D.
  • monomial_value.m, evaluates a monomial.
  • r8vec_uniform_01.m, returns a uniform pseudorandom R8VEC.
  • timestamp.m, prints the current YMDHMS date as a time stamp.
