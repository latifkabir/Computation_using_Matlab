TRIANGLE_WANDZURA_RULE
Quadrature Rules for Triangles

TRIANGLE_WANDZURA_RULE is a MATLAB library which can return any of six Wandzura rules for quadrature over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

There are six rules, which have polynomial degree of exactness of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30.
